I was driving home on Northfield Road around 8 PM yesterday when I started smelling something burning. A minute later smoke started coming up from under the hood. I pulled over and within seconds there were flames coming out near the engine. Got out and called 911. Fire department put it out but the front half of the car is basically destroyed — engine bay is gutted, hood melted, windshield cracked from the heat. The fire chief told me on scene it looked like an electrical fault, not arson or anything suspicious. Car was towed away — I'm assuming it's a total loss at this point.
