Came out of the Target on Brookhaven Drive Saturday afternoon and found my passenger side door dented in with a long paint scrape across it. There was no note, no nothing. I went back into the store and asked about it but they said their parking lot cameras don't cover that section of the lot. So I'm filing this as a hit-and-run that happened in the parking lot. Damage looks like maybe $1,500-$2,000 to me — the door panel is creased pretty bad in the middle and I think it'll need to be replaced rather than repaired. No injuries obviously, I wasn't in the car.
