I was driving down a narrow side street (Chestnut Lane) when a delivery van coming the other way clipped my driver side mirror with theirs. They didn't stop. My mirror is completely shattered — the housing is cracked and the glass is gone — and there's a long scrape down the driver door where their mirror dragged across the paint. Pretty minor damage overall but the mirror has all the side cameras and blind spot monitoring built in so I'm guessing it'll be expensive to replace. No injuries, didn't file a police report since the other vehicle was gone and I didn't catch the plate.
