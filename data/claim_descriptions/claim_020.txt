During the storm Tuesday night, a large branch from our neighbor's oak tree broke off and fell directly on top of my car in our driveway. The roof is dented in pretty significantly right above the back seat, the rear windshield is completely shattered, and there are scratches and gouges across the trunk lid and both rear quarter panels from the smaller branches. No one was in the car at the time, no other vehicle involved, no police needed for this. Filing it as a comprehensive falling-object claim.
