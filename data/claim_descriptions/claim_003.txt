I was driving on I-70 eastbound around mile marker 84 when a car in the left lane suddenly merged into me without signaling. It sideswiped my driver side from the front wheel well all the way back to the rear door. Two doors are scraped up bad with deep dents, the side mirror is hanging off by the wires, and the rear quarter panel has a long gash across it. No injuries thankfully. The other driver pulled over onto the shoulder with me, and we called the state troopers who came out and made a report. Both cars were drivable so no tow needed.
