Was traveling about 65 mph in the middle lane of I-71 southbound around 5:15 PM during heavy traffic. A car two lanes over to my left lost control and spun across the lanes, clipping my front passenger side. I tried to brake and steer away but I ended up hitting the concrete barrier on the right shoulder. So my car has impact damage on both the front passenger fender AND down the right side from the wall. Front airbag deployed. I was checked out at the hospital and released — just bruising from the seatbelt and airbag. Police and two tow trucks responded, official report attached. Car wasn't driveable, had to be towed.
