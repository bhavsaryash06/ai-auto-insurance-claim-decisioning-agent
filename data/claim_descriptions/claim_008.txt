Hit a deer on State Route 127 around 10:45 PM Tuesday night. It came out of the tree line on my right side and there was no way to avoid it. The front end took the brunt of it — hood is bent up, grille is shattered, driver side headlight is gone, and the bumper is hanging off on one side. There's also blood and fur stuck in the radiator area. Vehicle was barely driveable but I limped it home. No injuries, my airbags didn't even deploy. Called animal control and they took care of the deer. I don't think a police report is required for animal strikes in our state but let me know if you need one.
