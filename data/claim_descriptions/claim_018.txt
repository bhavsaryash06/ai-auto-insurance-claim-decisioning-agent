My car broke down on I-80 westbound around mile marker 38 last Saturday — transmission gave out completely, no warning. I pulled over onto the shoulder and called for a tow. While I was sitting there waiting, a semi went by in the right lane and the wind blast kicked up some debris that put a deep dent in my hood and cracked the windshield on the passenger side. So I've got both the mechanical breakdown issue plus the debris damage to deal with. Tow truck eventually arrived and brought it to my mechanic's shop. No police involved since it was a mechanical failure and there was no collision.
